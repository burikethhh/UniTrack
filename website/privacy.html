Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

Sultan Kudarat State University ("SKSU", "we", "us", or "our") operates the UniTrack mobile application (the "App"), a faculty location tracking and monitoring system. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our faculty tracking service:

Personal Data

  • Account Information: Name, email address (SKSU email), employee ID, department, and role (faculty, staff, or admin)
  • Profile Information: Profile photo (optional), contact details, office location
  • Employment Information: Department, position, office hours schedule

Location Data

Important: UniTrack collects precise, real-time location data of faculty members to enable campus-wide monitoring and accountability. Location tracking is active when faculty members are logged in and within campus premises. This data is used by authorized administrators and staff to monitor faculty presence and generate accountability reports.

  • Real-time GPS coordinates during active tracking sessions
  • Location history and movement patterns within campus
  • Building and room-level location data
  • Timestamps for all location records
  • Duration of presence in specific campus zones

Usage Data

  • App usage statistics and interaction data
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Login/logout timestamps

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To track and monitor faculty locations in real-time across campus
  • To verify faculty presence during scheduled office hours and classes
  • To generate location history and accountability reports
  • To display faculty positions on the interactive campus map for authorized personnel
  • To send notifications about schedules and tracking status
  • To improve the App's functionality and tracking accuracy
  • To ensure compliance with university policies and regulations

Who Can Access Location Data

Access to faculty location data is strictly controlled based on user roles:

  • Faculty Members: Can only view their own location history and tracking status
  • Department Heads: Can monitor faculty within their assigned department

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal and location information.

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Database access is restricted through Firebase Security Rules
  • Authentication is handled through secure Firebase Authentication
  • Location data access is logged and auditable
  • Regular security audits are conducted

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may be shared with:

  • University Administration: Authorized staff and administrators for faculty monitoring purposes
  • Service Providers: Google Firebase for data hosting and authentication
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or university regulations

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as you are employed at SKSU. Location history records are retained according to university data retention policies (typically for the duration of employment plus 2 years). You may request information about your stored data by contacting the IT department.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal and location data stored in the App
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • View who has accessed your location data
  • File a complaint with the university's Data Protection Officer

Note: As UniTrack is an institutional monitoring tool, faculty members cannot opt out of location tracking while employed, as it is a requirement for employment accountability.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
